About Us

As a prominent multinational in the field of education, WeTalk International is committed to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of traditional Chinese culture. We aim to enhance the global reach of Chinese culture through international Chinese language education, contributing Chinese insights to the advancement of the world and the betterment of humanity.

Branch and Student Distribution

WeTalk has set up branches in countries like China, Singapore, and the Philippines, and offers courses to students from a dozen countries including Singapore, China, the Philippines, South Korea, Australia, the United States, Malaysia, Vietnam, Japan, Poland, Venezuela, South Africa, Cyprus, Serbia, and Guatemala.
